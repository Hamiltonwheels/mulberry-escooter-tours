About Mulberry E-Scooter Tours

Small town. Big adventure. Zero emissions.

Born from a love of Mulberry, Georgia and a passion for eco-friendly fun, Mulberry E-Scooter Tours offers guided electric scooter experiences through the heart of our charming small town.

Our mission is simple: help locals and visitors alike discover the beauty, history, and hidden gems of Mulberry — all while gliding effortlessly on state-of-the-art electric scooters. No experience needed, just a sense of adventure!

Every tour is led by a knowledgeable local guide who knows Mulberry inside and out. With small group sizes (max 6 riders), you'll get a personal, intimate experience that big tour companies simply can't match.
