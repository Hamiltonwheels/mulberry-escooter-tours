Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your ride

No experience needed! Every tour begins with a brief training session where your guide will teach you how to safely operate the scooter. Our scooters are easy to ride and suitable for beginners.
Riders must be at least 16 years old. Riders under 18 must have a parent or legal guardian sign the liability waiver on their behalf.
Wear comfortable, closed-toe shoes and weather-appropriate clothing. We provide helmets and safety gear. We recommend bringing sunscreen, sunglasses, and a water bottle. Leave loose scarves and dangling accessories at home.
Our scooters have a recommended weight limit of 265 lbs (120 kg) for optimal performance and safety.
Light rain doesn't stop us — we'll provide ponchos! For severe weather (thunderstorms, heavy rain), we'll reschedule your tour at no extra charge or offer a full refund.
Free cancellation up to 24 hours before your tour for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable. No-shows are non-refundable. You can cancel online through the booking lookup page.
Our standard group size is up to 6 riders per tour. This keeps the experience personal and safe. For larger groups, please contact us to arrange a private tour.
Yes, all riders must sign a digital liability waiver before the tour. You can sign it online after booking — we'll include a link in your confirmation email. You can also sign at the meeting point before departure.
Our tour scooters have a comfortable cruising speed and are governed to a safe maximum for group riding. Your guide will set the pace based on the group's comfort level and the route.
Yes, free parking is available near our meeting points. Specific parking details will be included in your booking confirmation email.
